Game Instructions

  • A random number is displayed in the "Target" box.
  • 4 crystals are displayed on the page.
  • Clicks on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to the total score.
  • Player wins if the total score matches the random number in the "Target" box.
  • Player loses if the total score goes above the random number in the "Target" box.
  • All crystal values are hidden and will change when the game restarts.
